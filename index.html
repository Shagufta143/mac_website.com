Maharaja Agrasen CollegeEstablished in 1994 as part of the University of Delhi, Maharaja Agrasen College in East Delhi has evolved into a dynamic institution over two decades. Originally known as Co-Educational College, it later adopted the name Maharaja Agrasen College. The college offers innovative curricula in Arts, Commerce, and Sciences, promoting interdisciplinary learning and holistic development. With a learner-centric approach, education extends beyond classrooms to field-based research and community initiatives. Notable programs like The Leadership Development Program engage students in community upliftment, fostering strong bonds with localities. The college stands out for its commitment to comprehensive education and community development.
